### Historic British Census Data (1851-1911)

#### CensusGeocoder

[CensusGeocoder](https://github.com/Living-with-machines/CensusGeocoder) links addresses in digitised historic census data for Great Britain 1851-1911 (I-CeM) to their 'real-world' geographic location. [Read more](https://www.campop.geog.cam.ac.uk/research/projects/icem/) about I-CeM.

It was used to create [AddressGB Geo-coded British Census Addresses, 1851-1911](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.10473597)
